# Idea Refine

## Definition
Refines raw ideas into sharp, actionable concepts through structured divergent and convergent thinking. Interactive dialogue that produces a concrete markdown one-pager.

## The Three Phases
1. **Understand & Expand (Divergent)**: Restate as "How Might We", ask 3-5 sharpening questions, generate 5-8 variations using lenses (inversion, constraint removal, audience shift, combination, simplification, 10x, expert lens)
2. **Evaluate & Converge**: Cluster resonated ideas into 2-3 directions, stress-test each (user value, feasibility, differentiation), surface hidden assumptions
3. **Sharpen & Ship**: Produce a markdown one-pager with problem statement, recommended direction, key assumptions, MVP scope, and "Not Doing" list

## Output Format
- Problem Statement
- Recommended Direction
- Key Assumptions to Validate
- MVP Scope
- Not Doing (and Why)
- Open Questions

## Anti-Patterns
- Generating 20+ shallow variations (quality > quantity)
- Being a yes-machine instead of pushing back
- Skipping "who is this for"
- No assumptions surfaced before committing
