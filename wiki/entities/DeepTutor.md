## 📝 Overview

**DeepTutor** is an open-source personalized learning framework by **HKUDS** (The Data Science Lab at HKU). It uses a multi-agent architecture to move beyond simple chat-based tutoring into systematic research, problem-solving, and logic verification.

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Deep Solve**: Multi-agent pipeline for decomposing and validating complex problems.
- **Deep Research**: Systematic multi-source information gathering and synthesis.
- **Living Profile**: Persistent tracking of learner progress and styles.
- **Unified Workspace**: Integrated chat, Markdown co-writer, and code execution.

## 🧠 Connection to opencode-obsidian
DeepTutor is the "Logic" of the Agentic Trident, often orchestrated by [[n8n]] for autonomous research loops.

DeepTutor validates our approach of a **Persistent Learning Profile** (via Engram and `hot.md`). It provides a roadmap for **Logical Verification** beyond simple testing.

### Patterns to Adopt:

2. **Deep Verification**: Implementing a "Critique" phase in our SDD workflow to validate logical consistency.
